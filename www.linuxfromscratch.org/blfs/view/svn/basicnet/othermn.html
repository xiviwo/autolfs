Other Mail and News Programs

Balsa-2.4.12 is a GTK2 based mail client.

SeaMonkey-2.13.2 includes both a mail client and newsreader in its installation.

Thunderbird-17.0.5 is a mail/news client based on the Mozilla code base.

Evolution-3.6.4 includes a GTK2 based mail client.
